DND is the oldest known tabletop roleplaying game. In a tabletop roleplaying game, each player has a character that they pretend to be, and a character sheet of paper that contains the details of what their character is good at. The game master pretends to be everyone else the players interact with and sets challenges for the players to overcome, and together they tell a story. When the players attempt to do something difficult, they roll a dice and add any appropriate modifiers on their character sheet to the roll, and if the roll is higher than the difficulty that the game master set, the player succeeds.
